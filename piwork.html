Contributions

Computational Mathematics

Ramanujan's π formula provides a unique and efficient way to compute approximations of the reciprocal of π. This can be especially useful in numerical computations where high precision is required. It converges quickly, meaning that accurate approximations can be obtained with relatively few terms in the formula.

Ramanujan's formula can be used as a test case or benchmark for numerical algorithms, particularly those involving nested radicals or iterative methods.
