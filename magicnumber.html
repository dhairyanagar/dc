Magic Taxicab Number

Introduction

Taxicab numbers, explored by Ramanujan, are integers expressible as the sum of two cubes in two distinct ways. 1729 is the smallest such number (1^3 + 12^3, 9^3 + 10^3). These numbers captivate mathematicians and have diverse applications.
